The Montpellier Business Plan software is developed by Montpellier Méditerranée Métropole, based on methods and expertise offered by the Montpellier Méditerranée Métropole Business and Innovation Centre (BIC).

As a pioneer in the business incubation effort in France and Europe, Montpellier Méditerranée Métropole BIC is an internationally recognized startup incubator, designated as Incubator of the Year 2007 by NBIA, the international incubator network. The BIC has received several awards for its Web tools and software, including the DigiBIC Award in 2011 designated by EBN, the European BIC Network.

Over the past 5 years, Montpellier Méditerranée Métropole BIC has assisted over 500 startups with their creation and growth. Success has also been demonstrated by the survival rate of these companies: over 88% were still in business after 3 years (compared to Insee's evaluation of 63% nationwide in France), with 75% still active after 5 years.

Leveraging its Business and Innovation Centre, Montpellier Méditerranée Métropole is a unique contact for helping innovative companies reach success faster.

Since 1987, BIC has accompanied over 500 companies on the road to long-term success, with a three-year survival rate that is much higher than the national average.

The Montpellier Business Plan application was originally developed to help entrepreneurs assisted by Montpellier Méditerranée Métropole BIC elaborate their business plans. With the goal of sharing its expertise and experience as widely as possible, Montpellier Méditerranée Métropole BIC published the solution free of charge for all project leaders and entrepreneurs who need it.

The application is freely available in SaaS mode via the Internet, after the user opens an account on the site https://montpellier-business-plan.com.

By opening an account onhttps://montpellier-business-plan.com, the user may create up to 5 business plans online. He/she may decide, as appropriate, to offer business plan access to one or more guests, such as financial partners, shareholders, advisors, etc.
